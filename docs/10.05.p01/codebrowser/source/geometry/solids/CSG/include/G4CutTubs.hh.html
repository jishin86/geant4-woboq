33// G4CutTubs is a tube with possible cuts in +-Z.

35// from TGEo Ctube implementation (by A.Gheata, CERN)

37// G4CutTubs(pName,pRMin,pRMax,pDZ,pSPhi,pEPhi,pLowNorm,pHighNorm)
38// pName,pRMin,pRMax,pDZ,pSPhi,pEPhi are the same as for G4Tubs,
39// pLowNorm=Outside Normal at -Z
40// pHighNorm=Outsie Normal at +Z.
