33// G4Trd
34//
35// Class description:
36//
37// A G4Trd is a trapezoid with the x and y dimensions varying along z
38// functions:
39//
40// Member Data:
41//
42// fDx1 Half-length along x at the surface positioned at -dz
43// fDx2 Half-length along x at the surface positioned at +dz
44// fDy1 Half-length along y at the surface positioned at -dz
45// fDy2 Half-length along y at the surface positioned at +dz
46// fDz Half-length along z axis
47
48// History:
49// 12.01.95 P.Kent: Old prototype code converted to thick geometry
50// 17.02.95 P.Kent: Exiting normal return

52// 21.04.97 J.Apostolakis: Added Set Methods
