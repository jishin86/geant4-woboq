42// class description
43//
44// File for static data of PhotoAbsorption cross section coefficients
45// according to the Sandia parametrisation
46//
47// const G4double G4SandiaTable::fSandiaTable[981][5]
48//
49// These are the coefficients from F. Biggs, R. Lighthill 'Analytical
50// Approximation for X-ray Cross Sections III', SAND-0070, May 1990.
51//
52// fSandiaTable [n][0] - left energy borders in keV !!!
53// fSandiaTable [n][1-4] - Photoelectric cross-section coefficients
54// in cm^2 keV^(1-4)/g , respectively
55//
56// The first energy intervals and coefficients for Xe are corrected to
57// correspond perfectly to the data of J.B. West, J. Morton 'Absolute
58// Photoionization Cross-sections Tables for Xenon in the VUV and the Soft
59// X-ray regions', Atomic Data and Nuclear Data Tables 22(1978)103-107. The
60// coefficients are checked to correspond perfectly to the data from B.L.
61// Henke et al.,'Low-Energy X-Ray Interaction Coefficients:Photoabsorption,
62// Scattering, and Reflection' Atomic Data and Nuclear Data Tables, 27
63// (1982)1-144.
64// The coeficients for Carbon are checked to correspond perfectly to the
65// data of B.L. Henke et al. (as Xe). The first three energy intervals
66// and coefficients for C are corrected to correspond perfectly to the data
67// of Gallagher et al., J.Phys.Chem.Ref.Data, 17(1)(1988).
68// The coefficients for Oxygen are checked to correspond perfectly to the
69// data of B.L. Henke et al. (as Xe). The first two energy intervals and
70// coefficients for O are corrected to correspond perfectly to the data of
71// Gallagher et al. (as C).
72// The coeficients for Hydrogen are checked to correspond perfectly to the
73// data of B.L. Henke et al. (as Xe). The first three energy intervals
74// and coefficients for H are corrected to correspond perfectly to the data
75// of L.C. Lee et al., J. of Chem.Phys.,67(3)(1977).
76// The first energy intervals and coefficients for He, Ne, Ar, and Kr are
77// corrected to correspond perfectly to the data of G.V. Marr and J.B. West,
78// Absolute Photoionization Cross-section Tables for Helium, Neon, Argon,
79// and Krypton in the VUV Spectral Regions, Atomic Data and Nuclear Data
80// Tables, 18(1976)497-508.
81//------------------------------------------------------------------------------
